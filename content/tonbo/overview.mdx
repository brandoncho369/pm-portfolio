Tonbo is JDM watch-market intelligence for collectors — it scrapes Yahoo Auctions
Japan, Mercari Japan, and Yahoo Shopping Japan, matches each listing to US
secondary-market comps (eBay sold data, WatchCharts, Chrono24), and computes true
landed cost including proxy fees, domestic and international shipping, and import
duties. Subscribers get real-time Discord alerts when a listing is meaningfully
below the US market price; the free tier gets a daily email digest and a 5-model
watchlist. I built all of it solo — scraping pipeline, scoring engine, comp
collection, on-demand model research via Claude, and the subscriber-facing
dashboard. The cycles below show how I run it like a PM, not just a builder.
