Poker Ascent is a mobile poker trainer I co-founded and run product on. Most
study tools throw GTO theory at you — useless for the beginner and intermediate
players who actually fill live 1/2 games. We built a progressive tier system that
starts simple and adds variables as you improve. I own roadmap, prioritization,
and the beta-feedback loop; my co-founder runs distribution. The cycles below
trace how specific decisions got made.
